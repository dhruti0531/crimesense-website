About CrimeSense

CrimeSense is a demo web platform for reporting incidents, managing records, and exploring analytics—all in the browser. It is designed as a teaching/portfolio project showing best practices in front-end architecture.

Principles

  • Privacy: Data is stored locally in your browser (IndexedDB/localStorage). No server required.
  • Accessibility: Clean Bootstrap forms and components for great UX.
  • Clarity: Separate pages (no infinite scrolling) for focused tasks.
